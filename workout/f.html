Fun Day/Cardio Day

Choose an activity you enjoy doing. Basketball. Hiking. Etc. Perform for 70 minutes. If you do not have this option for the day, simply do cardio. To “spice” it up, perform body weight exercises within your cardio time. (Example: run for 70 minutes. However, every 10 minutes, you must stop and perform 30 pushups.)
