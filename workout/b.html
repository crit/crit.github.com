Axillary work for fast twitch fibers:

Choose 2 to 3 favorite back exercises. Perform 3 sets of each at 12 to 15 reps. (Utilize only 60 to 90 seconds rest here to engage fast twitch muscle fibers)

Bicep 'explosion': 100 reps of a bicep exercise for arms. (Find a weight you can only perform around 15 reps. Rest when needed. DO NOT let go of weight until all 100 reps are finalized.) Tabata work.

Tabata examples: kettle bell swings, jumping ropes, burpees, goblet squats, step ups (weighted), sprinting in place, high knees.
